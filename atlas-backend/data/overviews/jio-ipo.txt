Jio – Company Overview

What is Jio?

Jio is India’s largest digital connectivity company and one of the
world’s fastest-growing technology and telecommunications businesses. It
operates through Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited, a subsidiary of Jio
Platforms Limited, which is part of Reliance Industries Limited.

Launched commercially in 2016, Jio transformed India’s telecom industry
by making high-speed mobile internet affordable and widely accessible.
Today, Jio is more than a telecom operator—it is a technology ecosystem
providing connectivity, cloud services, digital entertainment,
enterprise solutions, artificial intelligence, and digital platforms.

What does Jio do?

Jio provides services across multiple sectors:

1.  Mobile Connectivity

-   4G and 5G mobile services
-   Voice and data plans
-   International roaming

2.  Home Broadband

-   JioFiber
-   JioAirFiber
-   Smart home connectivity

3.  Enterprise Services

-   Cloud computing
-   Private 5G
-   IoT solutions
-   Cybersecurity
-   Managed networking
-   Business communications

4.  Digital Platforms

-   Streaming services
-   Cloud storage
-   Digital payments
-   Gaming
-   Education
-   Healthcare solutions

5.  Devices

-   Smartphones
-   Routers
-   Set-top boxes
-   Connected devices

Purpose of the Company

Jio aims to build India’s digital infrastructure by providing affordable
connectivity and digital services while enabling consumers, businesses,
and governments to participate in the digital economy.

Business Segments

Consumer Business

Provides mobile, broadband, digital content, entertainment, and home
internet services to millions of users.

Enterprise Business

Offers networking, cloud, AI, cybersecurity, and digital transformation
solutions for businesses.

Digital Ecosystem

Develops applications and platforms for communication, entertainment,
finance, commerce, and productivity.

Technology Infrastructure

Operates nationwide fiber networks, data centers, spectrum assets, cloud
infrastructure, and one of the world’s largest standalone 5G
deployments.

Overall Takeaway

Jio has evolved from a telecom operator into a diversified digital
technology company. Its strategy combines nationwide connectivity with
cloud, AI, enterprise technology, digital platforms, and consumer
services. By leveraging Reliance’s ecosystem and continuing to invest in
next-generation infrastructure, Jio aims to play a central role in
India’s digital economy for decades to come.

IPO Status (as of 28 June 2026)

As of 28 June 2026, Jio Platforms Limited has not yet launched its 
Initial Public Offering (IPO) and its shares are not publicly listed on
the Indian stock exchanges. The company has filed its Draft Red Herring
Prospectus (DRHP) with the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI),
which marks the formal beginning of the IPO approval process. The DRHP is
currently under SEBI’s review, and the company is awaiting regulatory
approvals before announcing the final Red Herring Prospectus (RHP),
price band, subscription dates, and listing schedule.
