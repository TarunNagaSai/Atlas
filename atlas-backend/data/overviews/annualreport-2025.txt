JPMorgan Chase – Overview and Annual Report (2025) Summary

What is JPMorgan Chase?

JPMorgan Chase & Co. is one of the world’s largest financial services
companies and the largest bank in the United States by assets. Founded
in 1799, the company provides banking, investment, lending, asset
management, payment processing, and financial advisory services to
individuals, businesses, governments, and institutions across more than
100 countries.

The company operates under two major brands: - Chase – Consumer and
small business banking. - J.P. Morgan – Investment banking, commercial
banking, markets, and asset management.

As of the 2025 Annual Report, the company manages approximately $4.4
trillion in assets and employs more than 318,000 people worldwide.

What does JPMorgan Chase do?

The company operates through three major business groups:

1.  Consumer & Community Banking

-   Personal banking
-   Credit cards
-   Mortgages
-   Auto loans
-   Small business banking
-   Digital banking

2.  Commercial & Investment Bank

-   Investment banking
-   Corporate lending
-   Capital raising
-   Mergers & acquisitions advisory
-   Global markets and trading
-   Treasury and payment services

3.  Asset & Wealth Management

-   Investment management
-   Wealth management
-   Retirement planning
-   Private banking
-   Institutional asset management

The firm also processes trillions of dollars in payments every day and
safeguards assets for governments, corporations, and investors.
